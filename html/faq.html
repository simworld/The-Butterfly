FAQ

All parents receive daily feedback on their child's day. To keep parents informed and involved, we maintain individual diaries for each child. These diaries document important details such as their meals, sleep patterns, and the activities they participated in throughout the day.

We offer early starts and late closing (7am - 6.30pm, Mon-Fri).

We believe in the immense value of the outdoor play, which is why we ensure that children have the opportunity to play outside every day, regardless of the weather conditions. During the winter months, the children are warmly dressed, with wellies to splash around in puddles. In the summer, they wear hats and we apply sunscreen to ensure they can fully enjoy a wide range of engaging activities.

All meals are freshly prepared on the facility in our professional kitchen. The menu changes seasonally to provide a variety of nutritious and balanced meals for the children in our care. We happily accommodate vegetarian diets as well as any specific allergy or dietary requirements your child may have. We use only fresh meat and vegetables and all our dishes have a low salt and low sugar content.

At The Butterfly - Crèche & Childcare, we prioritize the qualifications and ongoing training of our staff members to provide the best care for children. All our staff members hold a minimum qualification of Fetac Level 5, with many having achieved Level 6 and above.

All staff members are Garda vetted and written reference are always checked and validated before any new staff member starts works in our facility.
