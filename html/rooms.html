Baby Room

Tailored for infants aged 4 to 12 months.

Our baby room accommodates a maximum of six full-time babies. Each member of our baby room team is a qualified Nursery Nurse with comprehensive First Aid training. We offer a serene and peaceful yet engaging environment that promotes your child's development during their crucial first year of life.

Wobblers & Toddlers Room

For children aged 12 to 24 months.

Our Toddler and Wobbler rooms are designed specifically for children aged between 1 and 2 years. In these rooms, we provide ample opportunities for children to learn and play, recognizing the curiosity that accompanies this stage of their lives. Through various activities such as art and crafts, music, drama, and tactile experiences, we foster the development of their senses, confidence, and concentration.

Pre-Montessori Rooms

Meeting the developmental needs of children aged 2 to 2.5/3 years.

The Pre-Montessori room serves as a stepping stone for children transitioning from the crèche to the Montessori program. We prioritize and encourage independence among the children, providing them with a supportive environment to develop self-sufficiency. In this room, we introduce the fundamentals of Montessori work, allowing children to enhance their concentration, gross motor skills, and fine motor skills. Potty training is a significant milestone during this stage, and our experienced team assists children in this important transition.

Our childcare facility also offers additional dedicated spaces, including:

  • Outdoor Play Area: A designed outdoor play area that offers a safe and engaging environment for children to explore and play. This area is equipped with age-appropriate play structures, encouraging physical activity, imagination, and connection with nature. Our focus on outdoor play promotes healthy development, social interaction, and a love for the outdoors.
  • Dining Space: Our dining space is a welcoming and communal area where children come together to enjoy nutritious meals. We prioritize healthy eating habits by providing well-balanced, child-friendly meals.
  • Sleeping Area: The sleeping area is designed to provide a comfortable and serene space for children to rest and recharge. Equipped with cosy sleeping arrangements, we ensure a peaceful environment that supports healthy sleep patterns. Our attentive staff respects each child's individual sleep routine, promoting a restful and rejuvenating experience.
